Frequently Asked Questions

Search through our standard policies, local weather details, and airport coordinates.

How do I reach the resort from Madurai airport?
Voyana Kodaikanal is approximately 130 km (a scenic 3-hour drive) from Madurai International Airport. Our reservations team can coordinate private luxury sedans or SUV pickups directly from Madurai or Coimbatore airports.
What is the peak mist season in Kodaikanal?
The majestic cloud mist occurs heavily during the monsoon months (July to September) and winter valleys (November to January). Temperatures average 12°C to 18°C. Every cottage fireplace is fully operational and stacked with wood for cold evenings.
Do you provide accommodation for personal staff/drivers?
Yes, we maintain separate premium staff lodging facilities on-site. Accommodations, basic dining arrangements, and parking facilities can be requested through your personal host during date finalizations.
What is your cancellation and booking modification policy?
We support full refunds for cancellations made at least 14 days prior to check-in. Modifications to bookings (such as dates adjustments) can be processed free of charge up to 7 days before check-in, depending on availability.
